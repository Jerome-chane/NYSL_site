About Our Team

The Northside Youth Soccer League was established in 1996 to provide athletes residing in Chicago's northside neighborhood an environment to learn and play soccer. To be a member of NYSL, you must be between the ages of 4-12 and reside in a Chicago northside neighborhood. NYSL is run by a small, full-time staff, and relies on the generous volunteed time of parents and former league members.
